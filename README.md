Bike Buyers Dataset Analysis

 - Project Overview
Explored customer demographics and lifestyle data to understand factors influencing bike purchases. The dataset included fields such as marital status, income, education, occupation, commute distance, and purchase behavior.

 - Tools & Techniques
Data cleaning, exploratory data analysis (EDA), visualization.
Trend identification by age, income, and commute distance

 - Key Insights
Higher‑income professionals with shorter commutes were more likely to purchase bikes.
Older demographics showed lower purchase rates.
Commute distance and occupation were strong predictors of bike ownership.

 - Outcome
Delivered actionable insights into customer purchase behavior, demonstrating ability to translate raw data into business findings.
